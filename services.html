Bookkeeping

Daily, Weekly, Bi-Weekly, Semi-Monthly, Monthly or Quarterly - Data Entry of purchase receipts/ vendor bills - Account Receivable & Account payables reports - Account reconciliations - P&L reports - peace of mind knowing that your Government Remittances are filed correctly and on time backed with detail reports HST, CRA, WSIB, EHT - Year end support with assembly of reports & liaison with accountants & gov agencies as needed. - Annual Payroll source deductions and WSIB reconciliation summaries - T5’s, T4A’s, T5018’s, T2200’s, T2125 Accounting needs for the preparation and filing of personal, self employed and/or Corporate tax returns are available.
